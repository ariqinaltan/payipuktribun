Hotel Fariones Review, Lanzarote, Spain

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Natural light, blissful comfort and sleek island-inspired design infuse the 213 rooms, which open onto spacious sun-soaked balconies with geometric-patterned floors, overlooking the pool or Playa Grande. Lanzarote colours (deep-sea blue, burnt orange) shine against sparkling-white walls, velvet furnishings and wooden floors, and every room has intriguing art.

The ultra-chic bathrooms have walk-in showers, marble countertops, glossy-turquoise tiling and theatrical mirrors, plus robes and slippers, professional hairdryers, and Rituals toiletries in full-sized bottles. Other touches include kettles, capsule-coffee machines, pillow menus, USB charging points and a sitting area. 

From Junior Suites upwards rooms include a cosy lounge; Suites have freestanding baths (some with ocean views); and the 10 Double Relax rooms get terrace hot tubs.
