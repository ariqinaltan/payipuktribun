Sephora's 5 Most Popular Lash Serums Are on Sale

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Sephora stores may be closed across the country, but that isn't stopping the brand from proceeding with its Beauty Insider Spring Savings event. And while there are tons of great deals to score (like marked-down Dyson hair tools, Nuface facial toning devices, and wildly popular Olaplex hair products), there are a few that are especially worth shopping if your eyelashes could use a little extra attention (quarantining is the perfect time to step up your beauty routine, after all!).

Sephora's bi-annual member-only sale is one of the only times loyal shoppers can save on customer favorites that rarely go on sale, including its top lash enhancers, growth serums, and conditioners. The retailer gave us the scoop on its five most popular eyelash serums, which all come with rave reviews from customers and are up to 20 percent off for Beauty Insiders.

To get in on the discounts, you'll have to join Sephora's free membership reward program. Rouge members will save 20 percent on their order now through May 1 while VIB level shoppers will save 15 percent through April 29. New members or Insiders will score 10 percent off their purchase from April 23 to April 27.
