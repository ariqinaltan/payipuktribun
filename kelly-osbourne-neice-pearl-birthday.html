Kelly Osbourne calls on baby niece Pearl to celebrate 30th birthday

Publish date: 2024-08-25

    Kelly Osbourne had a little helping hand when it came to ringing in her 30th birthday. The Fashion Police show host called upon her adorable niece Pearl, Jack Osbourne's daughter, to carry out the all-important task of blowing out her birthday candles. Sharing a heart-warming snap of the pair snuggled together, Kelly wrote on Instagram: "Thank you Pearl for helping blow my #birthday #candles out again!"

    Kelly Osbourne is doting auntie to little Pearl

    The sweet photo, which showed rock legend Ozzy Osbourne in the background, came a day after Kelly posted a slightly spookier image of her and Pearl again joining forces to celebrate Kelly's birthday, this time donning Halloween costumes. While the fashion designer looked almost unrecognisable channelling Magenta from , 18-month-old Pearl – who was dressed as a little witch – had the same expression of wonder as she stared and pointed at the giant pink cake. "Good bye 20's hello 30's," Kelly captioned the snap.

    Kelly Osbourne celebrated her 30th birthday twice with Pearl

    The purple-haired star seems to have had a whale of a time celebrating Halloween and her milestone birthday. No party would be complete without her nearest and dearest, and Kelly again took to the picture-sharing site to thank her "besties" for rallying around her. She also recently paid tribute to her late co-star and good friend Joan Rivers, who passed away in early September. In honour of 81-year-old Joan, Kelly had a tattoo of a bee etched onto her right shoulder. "If you looked at aerodynamics, at science, the bumble bee should not be able to fly," wrote Kelly. "Physically, it was just not anatomically equipped to soar. Yet it did, defying gravity, defying logic. The bee was a creature that defied and beat the odds, a miracle. Thank you for being my miracle @joanrivers."
