Nordstrom Slashed Prices Up to 60% on Designer Fashion

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Many love Nordstrom for its sales of massive proportions, even if it’s not convenient to scroll through the thousands of offerings at once. It’s a time commitment, to be sure — one that isn’t easy for all to fit into their day. That’s why I’ve done the work for you and rounded up the best deals from some of our favorite designer brands, such as Tory Burch, Veronica Beard, and Ugg.

We looked at thousands of deals so you won’t miss out on the best markdowns. Among the steepest discounts are a Vince’s wool- and cashmere-blend cardigan for 60 percent off and an Alice + Olivia’s plaid blazer for 40 percent off. Trust us, these deals are worth a spot in your cart, whether you’re starting your gift shopping early or simply indulging ahead of the holiday season.

Shop the 8 Best Fashion Deals:

Tory Burch Mini Ella Bio Tote

Norstrom’s sale section has plenty of playful accessories to gift yourself or someone you love. This Tory Burch mini tote bag is on sale in three vibrant hues — yellow, light purple, and light green. Made from a plant-based faux leather material, it's easy to carry as a handbag with the top handle or as a crossbody via an adjustable strap. Note: Many reviewers say they couldn’t fit much more than a cell phone, card case, and lipgloss in the bag, so it’s a great option for when you need just the essentials.

Coach Emilia Mary Jane Flats

Have a Mary Jane moment in these soft square-toe ballet flats from Coach. The wine color is 40 percent off and would look perfect with a pair of slightly cropped jeans (like these by Frame for 20 percent off) and a sweater or button-up shirt. Their leather construction offers a polished look that works well for both office and casual wear.

Vince Wool and Cashmere Cardigan

Layering is almost always a smart way to dress, as it ensures you’re never unprepared for sun, wind, or temperature drops after dark. This Vince cardigan makes feeling cozy look more chic with its clean lines and tortoiseshell buttons. Although the brown color is very versatile, we can’t help but love the brighter orange option, especially for the holiday season.
