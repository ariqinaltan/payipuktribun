Drama As Davidos 2023 Album Timeless Overtakes Wizkids 2020 Made in Lagos Deluxe,

Publish date: 2024-08-14
  • Fans of Davido and Wizkid have once again clashed over the singers' hit albums
  • Reports recently surfaced that Davido's 2023 album 'Timeless' had overtaken Wizkid's 'Made in Lagos' released in 2020
  • The lovers of the two music acts took to social media to react to the news as they took sides with their favourite
  • In an exclusive chat with Legit.ng, Kayode Folagbade, a fan of the two singers said both of them are unique in different ways

David Adeleke, better known as Davido, and his colleague Ayodeji Balogun, aka Wizkid, have been friends for a very long time but their fans will have none of that.

They have kept on comparing the songs and success of the two singers. They recently reacted after it was reported that Davido's 2023 album 'Timeless' had overtaken Wizkid's 2020 album 'Made in Lagos Deluxe'.

A report had surfaced on social media where top albums were showcased. According to the post, 'Timeless' is number 15 which was closely followed by Wizkid's album 'Made in Lagos Deluxe' which is on number 16.

Timeless overtakes Made in Lagos

Wizkid's album was the one leading a few months ago but within the space of a few weeks Davido's 'Timeless' took over the number 15 position that Wizkid was formerly occupying.

Some people were not surprised because the Grammy nominee had been in the news for some controversial reasons this year.

It started with his rift with Tiwa Savage, followed by the call out from TeeBillz and the petition laid against him.

Asake and Blaqbonez also made the list

Also sighted on the list was Asake's 'Work of Art' which was number 12, while Blaqbonez's song 'Emeka must Shine' occupied number 13.

Fans should stop comparing Davido and Wizkid

In a chat with Legit.ng Folagbade said there shouldn't be a comparison between the two singers.

He said:

"I don't understand why people are making comparisons between the two singers. Their kind of music, voice, and lifestyles are different. The funniest part is that their fans take it personal and engage in cyber attacks. The fact that Davido's 'Timeless' is ahead of Wizkid's ' Made in Lagos' album doesn't make either of the songs the best. People should learn to support their favorite in peace."

Wizkid and Davido's fan clash

Legit.ng also earlier reported how fans of Wizkid and Davido ignited fight and dragged each other online.

They went to X, formerly known as Twitter, to lash out at each other.

They also went as far as dragging the children of the two singers into their dirty argument.
