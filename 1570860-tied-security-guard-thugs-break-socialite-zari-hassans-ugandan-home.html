They Tied My Security Guard: Thugs Break into Socialite Zari Hassans Ugandan Home

Publish date: 2024-08-28
  • Zari Hassan was out when thugs broke into her Ugandan home and brutally hurt her armed security guard
  • According to the mother of five, the thugs stole her gun and other expensive things and wanted to drive away with her cars
  • She vowed to work with the Ugandan police until they find the people who took her licensed AK47 and hurt her security guard

South African-based Ugandan socialite Zari Hassan is heartbroken after thugs broke into her house and made away with her gun and other expensive accessories.

The mother of five shared the news via her Instastory, where she vowed to work with the Ugandan police to find who stole her gun and also brutally attacked her security guard.

How thugs brutally attacked Zari Hassan's security

She also posted a photo of her security guard bleeding after his head was beaten up by a gun and prayed that he would recover soon.

My family members are safe, and the security hopefully heals. They even had the audacity to ask where are the keys to the cars. And I think on hearing the police coming, they ran off," she added.
