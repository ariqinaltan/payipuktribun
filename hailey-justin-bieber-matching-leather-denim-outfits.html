Hailey and Justin Bieber Wear Matching Leather-and-Denim Uniforms

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Couples that match together stay together—and Hailey and Justin Bieber clearly got the memo.

Last night in London, the couple headed out in matching leather-and-denim outfits. Coordinating outfits isn’t necessarily a rare occurrence for the Biebers, and they both put their own personal touch on each of their looks.

Rhode founder and model Hailey opted for a black puffy zip-up leather jacket with a collar and pockets. She styled the outerwear with straight-leg light-wash jeans, plus black square-toe boots, a glossy emerald-green shoulder bag, black rectangular sunglasses, and chunky gold earrings.

Meanwhile, “Yummy” singer Justin looked cool and casual in an oversize black leather jacket, worn over a white T-shirt and baggy distressed jeans. He added a red-and-beige baseball cap, black sunglasses, and white sneakers to finish off the look.

The duo also coordinated their outfits on a date night in New York City last week. Then, the two matched palettes by stepping out in pale blue ensembles.

Hailey wore a pristine icy-blue blazer minidress from 16Arlington with a plunging neckline. She completed the look with white accessories, including a white Porsche baseball cap, a white woven Bottega Veneta bag, and ivory Jil Sander kitten-heel mules.

For his part, Justin again went the oversize route via a breezy pale blue button-up shirt worn over a white tank top, plus ultra-baggy cuffed jeans. He capped it off with a pair of bright orange shoes.

Hailey opened up about their marriage in an interview for Harper’s BAZAAR’s September 2022 cover story.

“He’s still the person that I wanna be rushing back to,” she said. “I might fly somewhere and go do a job, but I can’t wait to come back and hang out. And I feel like that’s because of the effort that’s been put in on both sides. At the end of the day, like, he’s my best friend, but it still does take a lot of work to make it work.”
