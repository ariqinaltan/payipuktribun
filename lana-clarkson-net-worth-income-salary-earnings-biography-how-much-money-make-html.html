Lana Jean Clarkson was born in Los Angeles, California, and died in Los Angeles, California. During the 1980s, she came to fame as a leading lady in a number of sword and sorcery films. Clarkson was tragically shot in the foyer of his home in February 2003 by composer and producer Phil Spector, who was there at the time.
