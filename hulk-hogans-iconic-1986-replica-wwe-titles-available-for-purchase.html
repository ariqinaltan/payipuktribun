Hulk Hogans Iconic 1986 Replica WWE Titles Available for Purchase

Publish date: 2024-08-25

WWE has been built on classic wrestling matches and moments that transitioned them from a regional promotion into the global juggernaut we know today. Moreover, they have decided to commemorate an iconic moment with a replica title belt of rich lineage.

The company recently listed a retro-style WWE Championship title for sale on its website. The belt represents the ongoing celebration of WWE Hall of Famer Hulk Hogan winning the title four decades ago.

The Hulkster went on to hold that title for an impressive 1,474 days during his first reign, which also saw him hold multiple versions of the WWE championship as he defended it across multiple top-tier opponents.

The aforementioned WWE replica title, having a deep relationship with the rise of Hulkamani has been listed on WWEShop for purchase at $499.99. It is based on the 1986 version of the title and is the first-ever memorabilia of its kind.

Description
Capture a bit of the Golden Era of sports entertainment and feel the power of Hulkamania by carving out space in your memorabilia collection for this Hulk Hogan 1986 World Heavyweight Championship Replica Title Belt. Modeled after the Championship held and defended by Hulk Hogan against Paul Orndorff inside Toronto’s Exhibition Stadium, this blast from the past features flags of multiple countries and intricate embossed details all along the strap. Perfect for any die-hard Hulkamaniac or sports entertainment historian, this retro prize is sure to call to mind one of the greatest feuds of a bygone era.

Hulk Hogan recently appeared on WWE television programming typing the 2024 Royal Rumble event and even teased about being an entrant in the 30-man over-the-top-rope melee. So it can be interesting if the fans see The Hulkster’s presence on the Grandest Stage of them all coming up in Philidelphia, WrestleMania 40.
