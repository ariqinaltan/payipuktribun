800 Movie Ott Release Date, Cast, Story &amp; Where to Watch

Publish date: 2024-08-11

“800,” the highly anticipated biopic of the legendary Sri Lankan cricketer and coach Muttaiah Muralitharan, finally made its theatrical debut on October 6, 2023. Directed by MS Sripathy, this film explores the fascinating life and career of Muralitharan, a member of the ICC Cricket Hall of Fame. Garnering decent reviews, the film successfully brings the cricketer’s journey to the screen. This article delves into the film’s storyline, its OTT release details, and the impressive ensemble cast that brought this biopic to life.

The Journey of ‘800’: From Concept to Screen

The biopic “800” offers a deep dive into the life of Murali, portrayed brilliantly by Madhur Mittal after the initial choice, Vijay Sethupathi, stepped down. The film navigates through various production stages and controversies, reflecting the challenges faced in bringing such a monumental story to the silver screen. It portrays Muralitharan’s journey against the backdrop of his cricketing career and personal life, highlighting the challenges he overcame.

The storyline of ‘800’ intricately intertwines Muralitharan’s professional achievements with his personal struggles. It sheds light on how he rose above the adversities and controversies that marred his cricketing career, especially regarding his unique bowling style. The film also delves into the cultural and political backdrop of Sri Lanka, adding layers to Murali’s story, and making it not just a sports biography but also a tale of perseverance and triumph against odds.

The film’s production journey is a story of resilience, mirroring its subject’s life. Despite facing casting changes and public controversies, the film’s team, led by MS Sripathy, remained committed to delivering a comprehensive and respectful portrayal of Muralitharan’s life. This commitment is evident in the film’s detailed storytelling, strong character development, and the portrayal of cricketing scenes, which has been lauded by both audiences and critics.

‘800’ on Jio Cinema: A New Platform for a Wider Audience

The digital debut of ‘800’ on Jio Cinema, starting December 2, marks a new chapter for the film, making it accessible to a wider audience. The acquisition of digital rights by Jio Cinema is a testament to the film’s anticipated success and its appeal to cricket enthusiasts worldwide. Streaming the film on an OTT platform ensures that Muralitharan’s story reaches not just cricket fans but also a broader spectrum of viewers who enjoy biographies and inspirational stories.

Jio Cinema’s platform provides a convenient avenue for viewers to experience the film in the comfort of their homes. This OTT release is particularly significant because it allows for a deeper engagement with the film’s content, as viewers can pause and reflect on the various aspects of Muralitharan’s life and career. Additionally, the digital release could potentially expand the film’s reach beyond traditional cricketing nations, introducing Muralitharan’s legacy to new audiences and cultures.

The film’s availability on Jio Cinema is also a nod to the changing landscape of film distribution and consumption. In an era where digital streaming is becoming increasingly prevalent, ‘800’ embraces this shift, ensuring that the inspiring story of one of cricket’s greatest players is preserved and accessible for future generations.

The Ensemble Cast and Crew Behind ‘800’

The casting of ‘800’ has been a crucial aspect of its success. Madhur Mittal’s portrayal of Muttiah Muralitharan is both compelling and authentic, bringing to life the nuances of Murali’s character. Mahima Nambiar, in her dual role as Madhimalar and Radhimalar, adds depth to the narrative, while Narain’s portrayal of Velupillai Prabhakaran and King Ratnam as Arjuna Ranatunga contribute significantly to the film’s historical and cultural context.

The supporting cast, including Nassar, Vadivukkarasi, Riythvika, and others, enhances the film’s storytelling, each bringing their unique flair to the project. The ensemble’s performance collectively paints a vivid picture of Muralitharan’s world, both on and off the cricket field, and helps in grounding the biopic in realism and emotional depth.

Behind the scenes, the crew’s contribution is equally noteworthy. Director MS Sripathy’s vision and handling of sensitive topics demonstrate a balance of respect and authenticity. Producer Vivek Rangachari’s investment in the project ensures quality production values, while R D Rajasekhar’s cinematography captures the essence of Muralitharan’s journey, visually narrating his story with precision and beauty. The editing by Praveen KL and Ghibran’s music composition further elevate the film, adding layers to the narrative and enhancing the overall viewing experience.

In conclusion, ‘800’ is not just a film about a cricket legend; it’s a story of resilience, determination, and triumph. Its release on Jio Cinema promises to bring Muralitharan’s inspiring journey to a global audience, immortalizing his legacy in the annals of cricket history and cinema.
