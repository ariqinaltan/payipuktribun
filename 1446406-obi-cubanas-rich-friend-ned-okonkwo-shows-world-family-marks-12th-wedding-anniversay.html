Obi Cubanas Mega-Rich Friend Ned Okonkwo Shows the World His Family As He Marks 12th Wedding Anni

Publish date: 2024-08-11
  • Business mogul Ned Okonkwo has warmed hearts on social media after showing off his family members in some adorable pictures
  • The heartwarming family pictures were posted in celebration of the wealthy businessman’s 12th wedding anniversary with his wife
  • Colleagues and other well-wishers in the online community showered congratulatory messages on the family

It is indeed a moment of joy and celebration for popular Nigerian business mogul, Ned Okonkwo, who recently celebrated 12 years of being married to his wife.

The businessman who is friends with nightlife entrepreneur, Obi Cubana, flooded his official Instagram page with beautiful pictures to mark the anniversary.

Ned, his wife, and their three children all rocked white outfits as they came together for the heartwarming family pictures.

The celebrants of the day, Ned and his wife, also came together for some loved-up pictures. He was seen placing a kiss on her forehead in one of the images.
