Title &amp; Steel Cage Match Booked For AEW Dynamite Next Week

Publish date: 2024-08-05

AEW brought a loaded show for fans this week on Dynamite, and next week is already shaping up to be a can’t miss week of action with the two matches they have announced so far.

Click here for our complete coverage of AEW Dynamite this week

AEW brought a big show this week, but they didn’t go off the air before the company revealed a couple of big matches for next week. They only announced two matches so far, but they could be show stealers.

AEW doesn’t break out their steel cage very often, but they will do just hat next week. Kenny Omega and Jon Moxley are set to tear each other apart in a match that is likely to get bloody.

The AEW International Championship will also be on the line next week. Orange Cassidy will continue his streak as a fighting champion as he puts his title on the line against Daniel Garcia.

We will have full coverage of AEW Dynamite throughout the week as Tony Khan’s company announces more matches. Keep checking back with Ringside News for more.
