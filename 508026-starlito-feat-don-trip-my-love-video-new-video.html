Starlito Feat. Don Trip &quot;My Love&quot; Video

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Frequent collaborators Starlito and Don Trip team up for the new video to "My Love," taken from Lito's highly underrated Introversion mixtape, released in May. The heartfelt record finds the two Tennessee OGs struggling in maintaining long-distance relationships while they're out hustling on the road. Their respective ladies think they're up to no good, but in reality, they're doing all they can to maintain a decent living. 

Both guys aren't ashamed to admit they've caught feelings, which makes it that much harder to persevere through the day-to-day grind of an underground artist. The record proves that Lito and Trip are two of the South's few remaining purveyors of "reality rap." With the new video, the pair announced that they are planning the third installment of their Step Brothers joint mixtape series. Stay tuned for a release date.
