Can linseed oil be used on wood floors?

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Answer

Linseed oil is a penetrating finish for hardwood floors that, unlike polyurethane, does not build a sealing on the wood, but rather functions as a stain to improve the appearance of your flooring. Because linseed oil is first applied as a saturating layer, it may penetrate open-grain woods such as oak and ash, allowing for a more natural appearance.
