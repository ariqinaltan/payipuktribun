The skin barrier, aka the stratum corneum, is the outermost layer of the skin, and functions as your body’s defense against pathogens, pollution, and other negative environmental elements. In doing so, the skin barrier keeps the whole body healthy. Moreover, a strong skin barrier is the foundation of a soft, smooth complexion, partially because it locks in hydration and prevents what’s known as transepidermal water loss. As dermatologist Dr. Stacy Chimento M.D., previously told InStyle, “Your skin barrier…keeps the water inside your body that would otherwise escape and evaporate.”  

On the flip side, a skin barrier that’s compromised — from harsh temperatures, over exfoliation, or certain conditions such as eczema — can render skin flaky, tight, and irritated, in both look and feel. 

Avène’s barrier cream is specifically formulated for compromised skin, though it’s safe for virtually anyone, including babies. The formula contains postbiotics to strengthen the skin’s microbiome — a collection of good bacteria that are crucial to barrier health — plus the brand’s signature thermal spring water, sourced from France and soothing on all skin.

The formula delivers immediate relief, calming redness, smoothing roughness, and alleviating dryness. Over time, it actually heals the skin barrier, in turn, lessening the severity of symptoms associated with barrier damage.
