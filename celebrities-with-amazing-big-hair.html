Celebrities with Frizzy Hair - Frizzy Hair Trend

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Since what feels like the beginning of time, we've been trained to banish frizz at all costs: Buy pricey serums, use hot tools daily, or opt for a bun as soon as the humidity rises. Well, forget that noise. On the heels of the bed-head craze, trust us when we say: Frizz is in. And we're embracing the sh*t out of it. Curls are fluffier and more care-free than ever, so look to these celebs for inspo and start cutting your hair some slack.
