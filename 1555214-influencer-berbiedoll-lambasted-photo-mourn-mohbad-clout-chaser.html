Influencer Berbiedoll Lambasted Over Photo She Used to Mourn Mohbad: Clout Chaser

Publish date: 2024-08-11
  • Nigerian socialite and Instagram influencer Berbiedoll has faced backlash online over the late singer Mohbad
  • The young lady got many people talking after she shared lovely photos of herself in a tribute post to the late music star
  • Many netizens wondered why she used her photos for the tribute, as they accused her of chasing clout

Popular Nigerian influencer Berbiedoll has come under fire over her tribute to the late Mohbad on social media.

A candlelight service and memorial was organised for the late musician on the evening of September 21, 2023, and several people turned up to celebrate the music star.

Berbiedoll took to her Instagram page to mourn the singer on the day of his memorial by sharing lovely photos and a video of herself accompanied by a sad message for Mohbad.
