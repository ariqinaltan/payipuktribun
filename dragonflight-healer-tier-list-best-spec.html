WoW Dragonflight S3 Healer Tier List (Jan 2024): Best Specs To Pick

Publish date: 2024-08-31

We rank all the Healer specs in World of Warcraft (WoW) Dragonflight Season 3 from best to worst with our tier list for both Mythic+ dungeons and raiding.

First Published January 2, 2024, 05:53

Want to be the best healer in the world...of Warcraft? Well, finding the best Healer spec in WoW Dragonflight Season 3 might not be the easiest thing in the world. That's why we've ranked the Healers from best to worst.

No matter if you are doing leveling dungeons, max level dungeons, Mythic+, runs, or getting into the raiding scene, our WoW Dragonflight Healer spec tier list has you covered, so let's get started.

Update on 2 January 2024: We've updated our Healer tier list for Dragonflight Season 3.

WoW Dragonflight Healer Tier List Ranking (S3)

The specs of the best healer in Dragonflight Season 3 aren't as clear-cut as it seems. One has to take the current metagame for Mythic+ and Raids into consideration, alongside the ability of healers to flash-heal tanks, AOE healing, and also DPS output.

We use the following rankings system for Healers in WoW Dragonflight. At the time of writing, there are no terrible specs that deserve to be in a C or D tier, but this might change as updates occur.

  • S Tier - The best Healer spec in WoW Dragonflight Season 3, pushing out a lot of group and single-target healing while still having the ability to deal some damage when the healing demand is lower.
  • A Tier - These Healer specs can still keep the group and Tank alive in the most demanding Mythic+ dungeons and raids but are simply outshined by the S Tier options on the healing meters.
  • B Tier - Don't expect to top the healing leaderboards with these specs in WoW Dragonflight. Even so, they can get the job done.

Now that you know exactly how we rank the Healer specs in this tier list for WoW Dragonflight, take a look at our picks below, which also takes data from Icyveins and WoWHead into consideration.

Healer Tier List in Dragonflight: Best To Worst

  • S Tier Healers - Restoration Druid, Preservation Evoker
  • A Tier Healers - Discipline Priest, Holy Paladin
  • B Tier Healers - Restoration Shaman, Holy Priest, Mistweaver Monk

So there you have all the Healer classes and specs, ranked from best to worst for WoW Dragonflight Season 3. We will endeavor to update this article once more balance changes occur.
