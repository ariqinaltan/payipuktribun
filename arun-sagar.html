Career

Notable achievements in the Kannada film industry have marked Arun Sagar’s career.

He began his journey as an art director, showcasing his exceptional skills in his debut film, Bhoomi Geetha, which won the National Award for Best Environmental Film.

Throughout his career, Arun Sagar has collaborated with renowned directors, including Poori Jagannath, Meher Ramesh, Veerashankar, and K Raghavendra Rao.

In addition to his art direction work, Arun Sagar gained widespread popularity for his comedic roles. He gained significant recognition by participating in the television show Maja with Sruja, where he portrayed comical characters parodying celebrities and politicians.

His talent for satire-comedy resonated with audiences, leading to the show’s immense success.

Arun Sagar’s comedic prowess was further highlighted when he became a contestant on the first season of Bigg Boss Kannada.

Although he emerged as the runner-up for the season, his charm and wit endeared him to the viewers. He later ventured into hosting shows like Comedy Circle on ETV Kannada and the one-person show Koyyam Kotra on TV9 Kannada.
