Who is Dr. MSN Reddy? Wiki, Bio, Age, Wife, Family &amp; More

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Dr. MSN Reddy (born on 27 August 1970) is an Indian-born Doctor, entrepreneur, Scientist, and Media personality from Telangana. He is popularly known as the Chairman, Founder, and Managing Director of MSN Laboratories Private Limited. He goes to many foreign countries for his brilliant work. 

He is in the headlines of the media because he has manufactured medicine to fight against Corona Virus. Moreover, he has also created opportunities for the employment of youth in technical sectors. He was honored with many awards by National and International countries.

Wiki & Quick Facts

Biography

Dr. MSN took birth on 27 August 1970. He was raised in Hyderabad, district of Telangana, India. He often visits different countries for the research of medicines and remedies. 

He has taken senior secondary education from his hometown school. He enrolled himself in the Osmania University of India for his Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Organic Chemistry. He started his career as a chemist and now he has his own business of drug manufacturing. 

Family & Caste

Dr. MSN Reddy has not shared any information about his parents and siblings. He devoted all his time to his studies and now he became a famous entrepreneur of India. As per Quora, Reddy is one of the most powerful castes so he holds Hinduism and belongs to the Kapu caste of the local chieftain class.

Wife & Children 

As per the reports of Deccan Chronicle, he lives with his wife named Prasuna, in a big Bungalow at Jubilee Hills. Sindhuja is his daughter and Achyuth is his son. The family lived together in a 17000 ft. villa. 

Career

Dr. MSN is the MD and founder of MSN Laboratories Pvt. Ltd. It was established in 2003, in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. This company considers as the most successful manufacturing and pharmaceutical R&D in the world. It gives the facility of home delivery to the patients at very reasonable prices. 

As per the official site of MSN Labs, It helped more than a thousand people by giving them medicines. Dr. MSN has also contributed medicine to the pandemic. He has launched an Oseltamivir 75 mg capsule called Oselow, which helps to get rid of viral flues.

Doctor MSN Reddy Manufactured Favipiravir Medicine for Covid-19

Dr. MSN has introduced a drug for the coronavirus called Favipiravir (Favilow) 800mg tablet. It is launched in April 2021 at Rs. 144 per tablet. The medicine was approved by the Drug Controller General of India (DCGI). It is a high-potency drug that contributes to fighting the virus.

Before this medicine, in August 2020, they produced Favipiravir 200mg and 400mg, which were lower in strength. This medicine holds the record of the cheapest medicine. It costs just Rs. 39 per tablet. Dr. MSN also serves medicines in foreign countries as well.

Net Worth

Dr. MSN Reddy is one of the wealthier entrepreneurs in India. According to the information of MSD Laboratories, they have grown from USD 22 Million in 2005 to USD 500 Million in 2019. Dr. is earning in Lakhs from his business. His net worth is estimated at INR 30-35 Crores including all income sources and assets. 

6 Trivia About Dr. MSN Reddy

  • He was honored with Udyog Ratan Award and FAPCCI Excellence in R&D Award in 2008.
  • In 2012, he received Customer-Centric Business Partner Award by Abbott Healthcare Pvt. Ltd.
  • He donated Rs. 5 Crores worth of medicines and equipment to the Chief Minister of Telangana to fight for the Pandemic. 
  • According to MSN Labs, they have 690 patents in India and Foreign countries. 
  • Dr. MSN Reddy has joined as Chairperson of the Board of Governors & Finance Committee of Indian Institute of Science Education & Research located in Berhampur in 2020.
  • A branch of MSN Labs has been opened in Myanmar in 2018.
