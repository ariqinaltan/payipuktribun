Never Before Look Of Kovai Sarala

Publish date: 2024-08-15

The anticipation for this film is only increasing with the release of each new poster and trailer. The first trailer, which was released on 3rd June this year, received rave responses mainly for Kovai Sarala’s never before look, grabbed more than 3 million views as of now. Kovai Sarala is known for her comic timing and extraordinary acting. She acted in various films across the Tamil and Telugu industries and is equally loved by both streams of audiences.

Actor Dhanush recently launched the second trailer of the much-awaited film ‘Sembi’ starring veteran actress Kovai Sarala. Sarala collaborated with acclaimed Tamil director Prabhu Solomon, popular for his romantic tragedy “Mynaa”.

Sembi, which garnered immediate attention from the audience for it has shown the Comedy Queen in a new light. Touted to be a visual treat, Sembi will hit the screens on the 30th of December as a New Year gift. 

She acted in more than 300 films, mainly in comic roles. But ‘Sembi’ is surely gonna show her intense and much more emotional side. Audience expectations of Sembi are quite commendable and they are eagerly waiting to witness the Queen of Comedy on the silver screen.
