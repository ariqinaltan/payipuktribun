Julia Roberts and her stylist Elizabeth Stewart wore matching Givenchy suits to a fashion party

Publish date: 2024-08-08

You might think that turning up to an event in the same outfit as someone else would be a fashion nightmare, but Julia Roberts wants you to think again.

The actress and her stylist Elizabeth Stewart arrived at the InStyle Awards in LA last night wearing identical lilac Givenchy suits.

The pair matched in every way, from their thick-rimmed glasses down to their pink shirts, white platform Oxford brogues and co-ordinating bags.

Related Story

According to InStyle, it was Roberts who came up with the idea when they were discussing outfit choices for the event.

“It’s not often we get to go to something together,” she explained, “and I said, you know, because we do look alike, ‘What if we wore, like, a similar outfit? Let’s wear the same thing.’”

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

They decided it would be fun as they are “kind of the same person,” Roberts said.

Not only did they co-ordinate outfits, but also beauty too, with similar relaxed waves and minimal make-up.

The suit featured in the most recent Givenchy spring/summer 2019 fashion show during Paris Fashion Week, worn by male model Jonas Gloer.

Also in attendance at the event was Givenchy creative director Clare Waight Keller, who has been nominated for the prestigious Designer of the Year accolade at the 2018 Fashion Awards.
