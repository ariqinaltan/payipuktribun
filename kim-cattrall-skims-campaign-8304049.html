Kim Cattrall Channeled Samantha Jones in a Sexy LBD for SKIMS's Latest Campaign

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Ladies, grab your cosmos, because Manhattan's favorite sexed-positive publicist is bringing some of her signature sensuality to a new SKIMS campaign. On Monday, the shapewear brand (founded by Kim Kardashian) released its new campaign starring Sex and the City's Kim Cattrall, who channeled her famed character Samantha Jones in a sexy all-black look.

On Monday, the brand released its Best of SKIMs campaign images, in which Cattrall posed in the black Soft Lounge Long Sleeve dress and a pair of classic pointed-toe shoes to match the dress. At a separate point in the shoot, Cattrall also wore the Sculpting Mid-Thigh Bodysuit, which she dubbed as her favorite piece from the company. Falling even more in line with her fabulous and stylish SATC character, Cattrall self-styled the looks. The images were photographed by Vanessa Beecroft.

“It was so freeing to be able to pick my favorite SKIMS looks for the campaign, and the piece I loved the most was the Sculpting Mid Thigh Bodysuit," Cattrall said in the press release. “It’s a phenomenal fit, and it makes me feel confident whenever I wear it.”

Cattrall was also joined by other legends and "next-gen" stars for the campaign including Lana Condor, Hari Nef, Coco Jones, and '90s pop sensation Nelly Furtado. Each woman styled their looks by choosing their favorite pieces from the brand. Furtado agreed with Cattrall, calling the Mid-Thigh bodysuit her go-to, as well.

“Styling all of my SKIMS looks was so fun, and my favorite piece was the SKIMS Sculpting Mid-Thigh Bodysuit,” Furtado stated. “The fabric is amazing and feels so good and secure on my body. It makes me feel excited about getting dressed, and I love working it into my wardrobe for both work and play."

As for the To All the Boys I've Loved Before star, Lana named the Cotton Collection as her fave. “I’ve always been a fan of SKIMS, so choosing my looks was easy," she said. "My favorite collection by far is SKIMS Cotton. It feels like it was made just for me, and the look I felt most comfortable in was the Cotton tank and underwear.”

While she's always been a staple in the pop culture zeitgeist, Cattrall garnered more attention than usual earlier this year when she announced she would be making an internet-breaking cameo in season 2 of And Just Like That ... She appeared in the season finale with a brief phone call with Sarah Jessica Parker's Carrie Bradshaw from across the pond. Despite it lasting just a mere 70 seconds and being filmed completely separate from any of her co-stars, the return of Samantha was certainly worth the wait.
