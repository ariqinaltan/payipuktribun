Do they still make Sweethearts?

Once made by Necco in Massachusetts for nearly 150 years, Sweethearts, the pastel candy “conversation hearts” stamped with sweet phrases of love and friendship, were long the most popular and best selling non-chocolate Valentine's Day candy. As of 2018, Sweethearts are no longer made by Necco.
