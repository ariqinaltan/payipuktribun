What exactly is JBCP?

The Joint Friendly Force Tracking System (JBC-P) is the Army’s next generation friendly force tracking system, providing Soldiers with a speedier satellite network, robust data encryption, and sophisticated logistics. JBC-P is an intuitive interface that contains capabilities such as touch-to-zoom maps and drag-and-drop icons. It was originally fielded to the first unit equipped with it in May 2015.

What exactly is the BFT army?

BFT, also known as Blue Force Tracking, is a critical situational awareness network used by the Army and joint services that provides friendly force tracking information and is integrated on more than 98,000 platforms. The Army is launching a long-term initiative to significantly upgrade this network.
