Actor and martial artist who played one of Jeffrey Dahmer's victims in the 2022 Netflix documentary series Dahmer - Monster: The Jeffrey Dahmer Story. He also appeared in series including Ryan's Mystery Playdate, Players, Pushing Skills, Gwen's World of Weird and Warrior. He was in the 2020 film The Paper Tigers.

He began learning karate at Rising Sun Karate in Stevenson Ranch, California, when he was five years old.

He made the Junior United States team and represented his country in international games. In 2016, he competed in Little Big Shots.

He was raised by his parents. He has sisters.

His costars in The Paper Tigers included Yuji Okumoto and Jae Suh Park.
