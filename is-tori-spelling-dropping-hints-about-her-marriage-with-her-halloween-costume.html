Is Tori Spelling Dropping Hints About Her Marriage With Her Halloween Costume?

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Tori Spelling apparently once again spent another calendar holiday without her husband Dean McDermott by her side. The reality television star shared a photo on her Instagram account of herself walking hand-in-hand with her friend Laura Rugetti as two dead brides. She captioned the post simply with an "I Do" and black heart emoji. It was enough for fans to once again speculate about what is really going on between Spelling and McDermott behind closed doors, much to his dismay.

Back in September, McDermott told the "Feminine Warrior" podcast that he thought all of the rumors about his marriage and supposed separation were "weird." According to Page Six, he said, "'What's going on with Tori and Dean? She's not wearing her ring.' Why do you need to know that? How is that going to affect your day? I don't reply anymore. It's just like, 'OK, if that's what you want to think, then think it.'"

Spelling and McDermott's marriage might not be dead just yet, but if Spelling's Halloween costume is supposed to be sending out a signal, it's that she might be ready for a re-do.
