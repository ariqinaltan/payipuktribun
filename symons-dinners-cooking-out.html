Symon's Dinners Cooking Out: Season 3

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Episode 1 Aired May 7, 2022 Mother's Chops Some of Michael Symon's dishes are influenced by the women around him; he makes grilled lamb chops with rosemary salt inspired by his mother, and his daughter-in-law's favorite dish off the grill is halloumi with watermelon kabobs. Details Episode 2 Aired May 14, 2022 Chicken and Waffles Gone Greek Inspired by his heritage, Michael Symon combines traditional Greek dishes with backyard American grilling; he makes sesame roasted chicken thighs and traditional baba ganoush; his wife, Liz, prepares tangy feta waffle chips. Details Episode 3 Aired May 21, 2022 Grilling All Grown Up Michael Symon gets nostalgic and makes childhood favorites worthy of a dinner party; he transforms humble mac and cheese into a creamy Marsala and mushroom pasta bake, makes rosemary bruschetta and shares boozy frozen cream bars with his wife. Details Episode 4 Aired May 28, 2022 Memories to Grill For Michael Symon connects food with memory, making grilled swordfish with salsa fresca that reminds him of heading off to college; for dessert, his wife, Lizzie, joins him to make ricotta cornmeal pound cake and share the memory of when they met. Details Episode 5 Aired Jun 4, 2022 Amping Up the Steaks Michael Symon uses coffee throughout his whole meal, starting with chicken-fried steak with pepper gravy that has a hint of coffee and some tobacco-tasting onions; his wife, Lizzie, shakes up a cold brew martini; banana espresso pudding. Details Episode 6 Aired Jun 11, 2022 Classic Cleveland City Chicken Michael Symon reinvents depression-era city chicken as cast iron pork tenderloin with asparagus and a tangy horseradish dressing; his wife, Lizzie, joins him to make foolproof mason jar strawberry ice cream. Details Episode 7 Aired Jun 18, 2022 Pop's Pancakes and Symon's Scallops Michael Symon makes quick marinated grilled scallops paired with potato zucchini pancakes with yogurt sauce; this light combination is perfectly satisfying, especially when enjoyed with pisco sours made by Michael's wife, Lizzie. Details Episode 8 Aired Jun 25, 2022 Anything Butt Bland Michael Symon takes the time to make a slow-cooked pork butt steak; he works on roasted cauliflower; Michael's wife, Lizzie, creates an all-American crepe cake. Details Episode 9 Aired Jul 9, 2022 A Tuscan Triumph Michael Symon makes Tuscan-style roasted chicken by glazing grilled chicken with floral vermouth and adding Brussels sprouts and kale salad; Michael's wife, Liz, adds green goddess dressing to the salad, and they toast with apple gin fizz cocktails. Details Episode 10 Aired Jul 16, 2022 Symon's Perfect Summer Supper Michael Symon makes a quick, flavor-packed and hearty meal that's perfect after a long summer day; he prepares cast iron striped bass with brown butter and lime cilantro sauce, and his wife, Lizzie, steps in to make a no-bake blueberry lemon pie. Details Episode 11 Aired Jul 23, 2022 BBQ Burger Meltdown Michael Symon can't help but make burgers when he fires up his grill; he works on beef and chorizo stuffed burgers and adds a side of charred broccoli salad with creamy dressing to contrast the char; his wife, Lizzie, stirs up peach sangria. Details Episode 12 Aired Jul 30, 2022 You Say Tomato, I Say Delicious Michael Symon makes a whole meal starring tomatoes; he starts with meaty stuffed tomatoes on the grill and also makes a delicate tomato tart tatin with sweet, caramelized onions. Details Episode 13 Aired Aug 6, 2022 Lizzie's Favorite Italian Feast Michael Symon and his wife, Lizzie, prepare an elegant Italian feast, pairing Lizzie's chicken cutlets with Michael's spaghetti with pesto tomato sauce; they top off the day with Lizzie's refreshing, fruity "C and C" slushy. Details Episode 14 Aired Aug 13, 2022 Family-Friendly Sunday Supper Michael Symon whips up a flavor-packed Italian dinner inspired by his college dorm days, starting with his one-pan pasta and a loaf of roasted garlic bread; Michael's wife, Lizzie, joins him to make arugula and fresh mozzarella salad.
