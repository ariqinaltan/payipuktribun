Na So: Wizkids Album, More Love Less Ego, Hits Over 200m Streams on Spotify Amid Rift With

Publish date: 2024-08-23
  • Wizkid's album More Love Less Ego which was released in 2022 has made a new record amid the fight he had with his colleague, Davido
  • The album has been streamed over 200 million times on Spotify as the beef he had with Davido made more fans rush to view his song
  • Netizens took to the comment section to slam him for using the feud as a strategy to rise while others praised the album

The X war between Afrobeat singers Ayodeji Balogun, better known as Wizkid, and his colleague David Adeleke, professionally known as Davido, seemed to favour the Made in Lagos crooner.

Legit.ng had reported that Wizkid took a swipeat his colleague, Davido, by using a controversial video of the OBO singer to reply his fans who requested for a new song.

Amid the development, the Grammy Award winner recorded a new feat on the popular streaming platform, Spotify as seen on social media.

His album, More Love Less Ego, also known as MLLE, has been streamed over 200 million times on the platform.

The album is the fifth work of the superstar released in 2022. It featured Ayra Starr, Naira Marley, Skepta, and other music artists.

It also includes song such as Money and Love, Balance, Sugar, and Balance to Me.

Recall that Wizkid had announced the release date for his MLLE album as he shared a teaser of the new work in 2022.
